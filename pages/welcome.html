Welcome to Adnota
Make Any Webpage Yours

Erase, annotate, draw, and resize anything on any webpage. Your edits stay on the page and come back next time you visit.

Adnota works on any regular website. It can't run on Chrome's own pages — chrome://, the Web Store, local files, or this welcome tab. Open any site in a new tab to try it.

The dock sits at the bottom of every webpage — it's on by default. Hover the A to expand it and pick a tool. Bare-key shortcuts (e s d r) work whenever the dock is visible.

The two grey icons at the right of the dock are Page Snippets (f) and Show / Hide All (Alt+S) — utilities, not tools.

Closed the dock with the ✕? Adnota goes silent on that site. Press Alt+A or click the toolbar icon to bring it back.

Two shortcuts worth knowing

  • Alt + A Hide or bring back the dock
  • Alt + S Show or hide all your annotations — peek at the original page anytime
  • Ctrl / ⌘ + Z Undo your last action

Pin Adnota for quick access

  1. Click the puzzle-piece icon in Chrome's toolbar.
  2. Find Adnota in the list.
  3. Click the pin icon next to it.

Local and private

  • No account. No sign-in.
  • Nothing leaves your browser. All edits stored in chrome.storage.local.
  • Open source. Code is on GitHub. (link coming soon)
